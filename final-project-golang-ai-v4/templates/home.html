About This Application

This application uses AI to help manage and optimize energy consumption in smart homes. By interacting with the chatbot, users can ask various questions related to their home's energy usage and get precise answers based on their data.

The AI model used in this application is fine-tuned to understand and process queries about energy consumption, offering insights and actionable suggestions to improve energy efficiency.
